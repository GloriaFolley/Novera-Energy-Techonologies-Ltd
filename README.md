Overview
Novera Energy & Technologies Ltd is a forward-thinking company focused on delivering innovative and reliable solutions in electrical energy systems, smart home automation, and security technology. We aim to improve comfort, safety, and energy independence for homes and businesses.

 💙 Mission Statement
To provide reliable electrical energy, professional CCTV surveillance, and smart home automation solutions that improve energy independence, safety, and comfort for homes and businesses.

💙 Vision
To become Ghana’s leading energy and smart-technology company and expand across Africa, accelerating regional digital transformation and the adoption of clean energy solutions.
