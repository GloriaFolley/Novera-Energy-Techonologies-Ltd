About Novera

Professional installers, trained and certified.

Novera Energy & Technologies Ltd is a Ghanaian security and access systems installer. We design, install, and maintain CCTV, video intercom, access control, automatic gates, and electric fencing for homes and businesses across the country.

Our team is Hikvision Certified Security Associates — formally trained and accredited to install Hikvision surveillance equipment according to manufacturer standards. Every project is executed with discipline, precision, neatness, and attention to detail.

Note: Novera is a professional installation company. We are not a distributor or reseller of Hikvision or Centurion Systems products.
